Welcome to Khaiyyara Jimbaran!

Situated in the Jimbaran Hill-Ungasan, with breathtaking view of Indian Ocean, Khaiyyara Jimbaran offers unique interior in blending the glorious Mediterranean Cycladic and Bali’s traditional village ambience with contemporary lifestyle. It is located just 30 minutes from Ngurah Rai International Airport. A collection of 30 thoughtfully outfitted rooms and 4 luxury 2 bedroom villas are showcased, with iconic 9 meters high water feature as its vital point. This 4-star boutique resort and villas neighbors to international beach clubs, world class tourist attractions, and splendid beaches.
